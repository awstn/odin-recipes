Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient. Other proteins and seafood can also be used. Beef, shrimp, fish are commonly used to cook sinigang.

Ingredients

  • 2lb Pork Shoulder (or any other protein
  • Kang kong
  • Okra
  • Thai Chili
  • Long chili
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Tamarind
  • Long beans
  • Fish Sauce
  • Daikon

Steps

  1. Optional: If using meat, sear it.
  2. Pour water and add fish sauce. Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and onions. Daikon as well if using.
  4. Add enough water to cover everything. Let it simmer for an hour.
  5. Add Okra and chili. Let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Wait until meat is fork tender.
  7. Add kang kong and cook for another 3 - 5 minutes.
  8. Add tamarind seasoning or paste and let it cook for another 3 minutes.
  9. Taste and add more fish sauce if need be.
